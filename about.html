About Personal Bank

Building financial futures since 1985 with trust, innovation, and personalized service

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Personal Bank began as a small community bank with a simple mission: to provide personalized financial services that help individuals and businesses thrive.

Over the decades, we've grown to serve customers across the nation while maintaining our commitment to the personal touch that set us apart from the beginning. Our journey has been marked by steady growth, technological innovation, and an unwavering focus on customer satisfaction.

Today, we manage over $50 billion in assets and serve more than 2 million customers, but we still operate with the same community-focused values that guided our founders.

Community Involvement

We believe in giving back to the communities that have supported our growth.

Each year, Personal Bank contributes over $5 million to local charities, educational programs, and community development initiatives. Our employees volunteer thousands of hours annually, and we offer special banking programs for non-profit organizations.

Through our "Community First" initiative, we've helped fund the construction of 15 community centers, provided scholarships to over 500 students, and supported small business development in underserved areas.
